## Head of Software Engineering

Ioannis has 20 years of experience as a software engineer, 15 of which as a full time open source developer.
He is a member of the [Apache Software Foundation](http://www.apache.org), with contributions to numerous projects as a committer and PMC member.

He is currently Head of Software Engineering at [Cyber Ranges](https://cyberranges.com) (2025 -- present).
Previously, he spent over a decade at [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com) (2012 -- 2025), most recently as Senior Principal Software Engineer on the [Quarkus](https://quarkus.io) team, working on cloud-native integrations with [Kubernetes](https://kubernetes.io) and [OpenShift](https://www.openshift.com).

He is the creator of [Spring Cloud Kubernetes](https://github.com/spring-cloud/spring-cloud-kubernetes), [Fabric8 Kubernetes Client](https://github.com/fabric8io/kubernetes-client), [Dekorate](https://github.com/dekorateio/dekorate), [Sundrio](https://github.com/sundrio/sundrio), and [Apache Karaf Cellar](https://github.com/apache/karaf-cellar).
