I am Ioannis, father, software engineer, recreational athlete, but chances are you are here for my professional capacity, so here goes:

I build developer tools, technologies and workflows that make software engineering fun. I believe simplicity beats sophistication and that's not just a preference, it's history repeating itself over and over again.

I have over 21 years of experience in the field, 15 of which as a full time open source developer. I'm a member of the [Apache Software Foundation](http://www.apache.org), contributing to numerous projects as a committer and PMC member. Currently, I'm Head of Software Engineering at [Cyber Ranges](https://cyberranges.com) (2025 -- present). Before that, I spent over a decade at [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com) (2012 -- 2025), most recently as Senior Principal Software Engineer on the [Quarkus](https://quarkus.io) team, working on cloud native integrations with [Kubernetes](https://kubernetes.io) and [OpenShift](https://www.openshift.com).

Along the way I've created [Spring Cloud Kubernetes](https://github.com/spring-cloud/spring-cloud-kubernetes), [Fabric8 Kubernetes Client](https://github.com/fabric8io/kubernetes-client), [Dekorate](https://github.com/dekorateio/dekorate), [Sundrio](https://github.com/sundrio/sundrio) and [Apache Karaf Cellar](https://github.com/apache/karaf-cellar).

I enjoy writing and speaking about the same things I love building, whatever gets me professionally excited at the time.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me on the mats training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. I love that it combines the mental with the physical and that your personality goes into your game, making it as unique as a fingerprint. It's an ever changing sport so vast that no two people play it the same way.
